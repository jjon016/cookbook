Chicken Pot Pie

Submitted by Joshua Jones

Recipe tried by 21 different people

Lets Make It!
Ingredients
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 3 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Lbs Chicken Breast
  • 1 Cup Frozen Carrot/Pea Mix
  • 1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 2 Small Potatoes
  • 2 Pie Crust
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • Salt/Pepper to Taste
  • 1 Tsp Thyme
  • 1 Tsp Rosemary
Directions

Preheat oven to 350. Spray a deep dish pie pan with cooking spray. Roll pie crusts out and place one crust in pie pan. Saute onion in a pan with a little bit of oil and/or button. Add garlic to onions once they begin to brown. Saute for another minute and add the potatoes along with a cup or two of water. Cook potatoes for 10 minutes until most of the water has boiled off. Combine the mixed vegetables, cream of chicken, milk, and spices in the pan. Stir until everything is incorperated and then add to the pie dish. Place the remaining pie crust on top and crimp the edges of the crusts so they are combined. Slice a couple holes on top of the crust so it can vent the steam when it is cooking. Place in the oven and cook for 45 minutes.
